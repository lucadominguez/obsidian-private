## Layout: dashboard
## Style: technical-schematic (blueprint variant)
## Aspect: landscape (16:9)
## Language: English

---

Dashboard layout with 12 metric widgets arranged in a 4×3 grid. Each widget shows one blood marker as a gauge-style visualization with standard range vs optimal range. Technical schematic style — blueprint variant: white text and lines on a deep navy blue (#1E3A5F) background with subtle grid pattern. Amber (#F59E0B) accents for important callouts and trend arrows.
