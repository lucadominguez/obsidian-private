# Claims Warfare

> **Publication premise:** A former insurance adjuster reveals exactly how the claims game works — what they're trained to look for, deny, and lowball — and gives policyholders the playbook to win. Not general advice. Specific, actionable intelligence from someone who sat on the other side of the desk.

## Why This Works

Insurance claims are:
- **Universal** — everyone has insurance, most people will file a claim eventually
- **High-stakes** — $5,000 to $500,000+ hangs in the balance
- **Time-bound** — claims have hard deadlines, creating urgency
- **Asymmetric information** — the adjuster knows the game. The policyholder doesn't.
- **Emotionally charged** — people are angry, scared, and looking for someone who actually knows what they're talking about

The content bridges the information gap. Free content proves the expertise. Paid content delivers the playbook.
