## The Decision Framework

```
YOU HAVE DEBT
    │
    ├── Past SOL? → YES → IGNORE (legally dead)
    │
    └── NO → Send Validation Letter (FDCPA §1692g)
                  │
                  ├── Can't validate (70%) → DEBT IS DEAD
                  │
                  └── CAN validate → Negotiate Settlement
                                          │
                                          ├── On credit report? → Dispute / Pay-for-delete
                                          │
                                          └── Clean → Rebuild (secured card, <10% util)
```

## The Weapons — In Order

| Priority | Weapon | When | Cost | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1st | Statute of Limitations | Debt 3-6+ years old | $0 | 100% of balance |
| 2nd | Debt Validation Letter | Within 30 days of contact | ~$5 | 100% (if unvalidated) |
| 3rd | Settlement Negotiation | Debt is valid & collectible | 25-40% | 60-75% of balance |
| 4th | Credit Report Dispute | Negative items on report | ~$5/letter | 40+ pts per item |
| 5th | Pay-for-Delete | Verified debt, clean report | Negotiated | Complete removal |
