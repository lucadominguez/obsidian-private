# Free vs Paid Strategy — Debt Elimination

## The Core Principle

> Free content proves you know the system. Paid content is the template library and strategy database.

## What Goes Where

### Always Free
- Strategy overviews ("what's possible")
- Personal story and results
- Debt collector rights education
- Credit score fundamentals
- Single template previews

### Always Paid
- Full template library (validation letters, settlement offers, disputes)
- Creditor intelligence database (who settles for what %)
- Personal debt audit and strategy
- Credit report review
- Communication coaching
- Bankruptcy navigation

## The Paywall Moment

Subscribers pay for:

1. **Templates** — Copy-paste ready. The exact language that worked.
2. **Intelligence** — Which creditors fold at 30%, which fight to the end.
3. **Strategy** — What to pay, what to settle, what to ignore. The decision tree.
4. **Accountability** — A community keeping you on track.
