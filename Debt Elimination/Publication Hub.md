## Why This Works

Consumer debt is:
- **Massive** — $1.7 trillion in US consumer debt. Millions of people losing sleep.
- **Emotionally devastating** — debt collectors, wage garnishment, credit score tanking — people feel trapped
- **Information-asymmetric** — creditors know the rules. Debtors don't.
- **Template-heavy** — content is validation letters, settlement scripts, dispute templates. Highly automatable.
- **Results-driven** — when someone settles $14K for $4,200 using your template, they're a customer for life.
