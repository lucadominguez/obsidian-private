## Content Tiers

### Free Tier (Top of Funnel)
**Goal:** Prove you've been there. Share insights that make people feel understood for the first time. Create the "she actually gets it — what else does she know?" moment.

| Content Type | Example | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Personal story | "My 3-Year Recovery Timeline with ME/CFS" | Vulnerability + hope. The emotional core. |
| Lab test reveals | "The Blood Tests Your Doctor Isn't Ordering" | Actionable, shocking. Most patients have never heard of these. |
| Doctor experiences | "How I Found a Doctor Who Believed Me" | Universal pain point. Everyone has been gaslit. |
| Protocol teasers | "Low-Dose Naltrexone: What 6 Months of Data Shows" | Specific data builds credibility. |
| Medical gaslighting | "5 Things Doctors Said to Me (And Why They Were Wrong)" | Emotional hook + validation. Highly shareable. |
| Daily life strategies | "Pacing: The Hardest Treatment (And Why It Works)" | Practical. Immediate value. |

### Paid Tier (Behind Paywall)
**Goal:** The protocol database. The doctor recommendation list. The community support. Worth it because the alternative is suffering alone.

## Welcome Sequence (6 emails, 10 days)

1. **Day 0:** "You're not alone. Here's where to start." — Deliver lead magnet
2. **Day 2:** "The tests most doctors miss" — Immediate value
3. **Day 4:** "My 3-year recovery timeline" — Personal story + hope
4. **Day 7:** "What 200 patients taught me about LDN" — Data + credibility
5. **Day 9:** "Is it worth $99/month?" — Objection handling
6. **Day 10:** "Join the community" — Soft close
