## Usage Notes

- Midjourney: Add `--style raw --ar 1:1 --v 6.1` for most photorealistic results
- DALL-E 3: Use natural language mode, square aspect
- ComfyUI SDXL: Add "photorealistic, 8k, high detail, professional lighting" as positive prompts
- These are designed to match real headshots, not AI-generated-looking portraits
- Each prompt includes environmental details that tell the persona's story
