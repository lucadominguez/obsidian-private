# Free vs Paid Strategy — Credential Engineering

## The Core Principle

> Free content proves the pathways exist. Paid content is the GPS that gets you there.

## What Goes Where

### Always Free
- Pathway spotlights ("did you know you can do this?")
- Industry exposes ("the accredited degree that costs $4K")
- Success stories with redacted details
- General credential evaluation guidance
- Career maps showing what's possible

### Always Paid
- State-by-state licensing pathway database
- Specific forms, fees, and application timelines
- Employer negotiation scripts
- Personal pathway design
- Direct contact information for credential evaluators
- Immigration-specific career pathways

## The Paywall Moment

Subscribers pay for:

1. **Speed** — Stop spending weeks researching 50 state websites. We already did it.
2. **Certainty** — Exact forms, exact fees, exact timelines. No guessing.
3. **The hidden pathways** — Licensing loopholes and challenge exams nobody talks about.
4. **The scripts** — What to say when an employer questions your background.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "$99/month seems high" | "One unnecessary college course costs $2,000. One year of delayed career advancement costs $30-80K. This is the cheapest career accelerator you'll ever buy." |
| "I can just Google this" | "The information exists across 50 state websites, buried in PDFs. We spent hundreds of hours centralizing it. Your time is worth more." |
| "What if my profession isn't covered?" | "The database covers 40+ professions. If yours isn't here yet, ask — we research on demand for Pro and VIP members." |
